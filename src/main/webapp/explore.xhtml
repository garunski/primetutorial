Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics

People working in this field solve problems through research, design and testing of products and services. They find new and better ways of doing things in fields like mathematics, computers, economics, and the physical, social, life and human sciences.

Health Sciences

People working in this field promote health and wellness. They diagnose and treat injuries and disease. As researchers and scientists, they investigate processes, and study the nature of disease, its cause, transmission, and effect on the body.

Business & Finance

People working in this field focus on planning, organizing and evaluating business functions. They may develop, track and disperse funds; be involved in buying, selling or servicing; or perform administrative, financial and data processing operations.

Arts & Humanities

People working in this field engage in literary, cultural, artistic, athletic and entertainment activities. They interpret and communicate concepts and ideas through writing, painting, drawing, speaking, dancing, musical and other communication techniques.
